maven由于他的约定大于配置，我们之后可能会遇到我们写的配置文件，无法导出或无法生效的问题


解决方案：

https://www.cnblogs.com/pixy/p/4798089.html



<resources>

<resource>

<directory>src/main/java</directory>

<includes>

<include>**/*.properties</include>

<include>**/*.xml</include>

</includes>

<filtering>false</filtering>

</resource>

</resources>
